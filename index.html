Publications

  • Detection of neovascularization in retinal images using semi-supervised learning (ISBI)

    This work proposes a semi-supervised, patch-based framework that leverages both labeled and unlabeled data. It fuses feature representations with neighborhood information and applies rule-based scoring to make image-level decisions, enabling more scalable and reliable detection.

    View on Google Scholar
  • Multimodal Registration of Retinal Images (NCVPRIPG)

    Registration across different imaging modalities is difficult due to noise, projection differences, and limited overlap. Existing methods depend on accurate structure segmentation or key-point detection, which are unreliable in such conditions. We propose a landmark-based algorithm that works effectively without these strict requirements.

    View on Google Scholar
  • Density measurement using OCT-A in early glaucoma (Journal of Glaucoma)

    This study compares capillary density between normal subjects and early glaucoma patients using image-based analysis. A filtering method is applied to measure density across sectors, which is then statistically compared with structural and functional indicators.

    View on Google Scholar
